## Migration Guidance

When moving or translating concepts out of the Notion archive:

1. Identify the conceptual domain.
2. Place the refined version into the correct WORKING_DOCS folder.
3. Keep the original archive untouched.
4. Prefer synthesis over duplication.
5. Use clear filenames that describe the system or concept directly.
