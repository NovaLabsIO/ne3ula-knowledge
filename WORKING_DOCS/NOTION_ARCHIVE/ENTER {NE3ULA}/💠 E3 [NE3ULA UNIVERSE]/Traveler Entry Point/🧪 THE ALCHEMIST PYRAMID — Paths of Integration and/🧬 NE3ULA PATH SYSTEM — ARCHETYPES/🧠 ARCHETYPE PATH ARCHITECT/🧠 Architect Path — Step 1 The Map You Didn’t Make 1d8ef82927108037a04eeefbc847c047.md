# 🧠 Architect Path — Step 1: The Map You Didn’t Make

> “You were handed a map — but you were never asked where you actually wanted to go.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> Before you can build with purpose, you have to question what you’ve inherited.
> 
> 
> The culture, the timelines, the expectations — systems built by others for other ends.
> 
> **The Map You Didn’t Make** reveals the first truth of the Architect: before you can design *for* yourself, you must learn to see what’s been designing *you.*
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- Imagine the world you live in as a machine. Who built it — and for what purpose?
- What ancient cities or mythic systems do you admire — or distrust?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What structures, beliefs, or institutions currently shape your decisions — even unconsciously?
- What parts of your inner or outer world were built for survival rather than flourishing?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- How does it feel to realize that many parts of your identity may have been constructed without your full awareness?
- What emotions arise when you realize the “map” you’ve followed isn’t aligned with where you truly want to go?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- Are you willing to challenge the blueprints you’ve inherited — not out of rebellion, but out of respect for truth?
- What is your responsibility now that you see the design was never neutral?
