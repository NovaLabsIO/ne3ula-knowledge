# 🧭 Seeker Path — Step 2: The Empty Horizon

> “The unknown is not empty. It is waiting for you to arrive.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> To seek is to leave something behind.
> 
> 
> Not everything you carry will make it across the horizon.
> 
> **The Empty Horizon** is the first real confrontation:
> 
> the vastness that humbles and invites you beyond old boundaries.
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- Imagine standing at the edge of your known life. What lies beyond it?
- What mythic heroes or figures do you feel standing beside you at this threshold?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What systems, routines, or identities are you afraid to lose?
- What skills or strengths will you carry across the unknown?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- What emotions rise when you face the possibility of losing the familiar?
- How does hope feel different from expectation in your body?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- Are you willing to let go of what no longer fits the journey — even if it once saved you?
- What would it mean to trust the unknown as part of your becoming?
