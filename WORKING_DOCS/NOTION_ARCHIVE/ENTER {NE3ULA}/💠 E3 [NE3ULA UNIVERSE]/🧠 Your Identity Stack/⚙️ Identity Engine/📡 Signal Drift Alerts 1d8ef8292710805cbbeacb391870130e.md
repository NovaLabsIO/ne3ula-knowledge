## 🔎 What is Signal Drift?

Signal Drift is the **subtle separation between your identity and your actions**.

It’s when your rituals, choices, or words begin to **echo an older version of you** — or someone else entirely.

It often starts quietly:

- You stop doing the things that center you
- You say “yes” when your body says “no”
- You feel foggy, flat, or over-performing
- You go autopilot in the name of “being productive”

> Drift isn’t failure.
> 
> 
> It’s just a sign that **reconnection is due.**
> 

---

## 🧭 Early Indicators of Drift

Use this list to self-check — or allow AI.d to learn your unique signals.

### 😶 Emotional Flattening

- You feel muted — less joy, less anger, less anything
- Your creative work feels hollow or forced

### 😤 Internal Dissonance

- You’re doing “the right things,” but they don’t feel like *yours*
- You start resenting tasks or people you usually love

### 🌀 Ritual Collapse

- You stop doing your small rituals — not because they don’t work, but because you forgot why they matter
- You start “just checking your phone” more than usual

### ⏳ Time Distortion

- You feel disconnected from time — either racing through days or stuck in loops

## 🛠 Manual Check-In Prompts

Use these when something feels “off” — even if you can’t name it.

- What am I doing lately that doesn’t feel like me?
- What part of my Identity Core have I unintentionally disconnected from?
- Is this moment a reflection of my chosen signal — or something I’ve unconsciously borrowed or revived?
- What would realignment feel like right now?

📝 *Journal, voice memo, or mark the drift moment in a log.*
