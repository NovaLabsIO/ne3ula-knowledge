# 🧬 Identity Core

*The authored center of your NE3ULA signal.*

---

## 📍 What is the Identity Core?

The Identity Core is the **deepest layer of your personal system** — the part you choose, not the part you inherited.

It’s where you define your **truth**, your **boundaries**, your **creative intent**, and your **self-expression**.

This is your inner compass — the code that everything else is built around.

---

## 🧱 Your 4 Protocols of Identity

These four pillars form the foundation of your personal signal.

You can return to them, rewrite them, or refine them as often as you need.

They are not who you are — they are how you choose to live.

---

### 💰 a. Get Your Money Right

**Essence**: Energetic sovereignty through financial clarity

- What does financial freedom mean to you — *truly*?
- Where are you spending energy just to survive, instead of to create?
- What would it feel like to trust your income, your rhythm, and your worth?

📝 *Write your personal money philosophy and action commitments here.*

---

### 🤝 b. Rules of Engagement

**Essence**: Conscious interaction with people, systems, and the world

- How do you want to show up in relationships, conflict, and collaboration?
- What behaviors will you no longer tolerate — from yourself or others?
- What does respect look and feel like for you?

📝 *List your personal Rules of Engagement here — this is your internal code of conduct.*

---

### 🛠 c. Build What You Wish the World Had

**Essence**: Create the signal only you can broadcast

- What problem, vision, or pattern are you here to challenge or replace?
- What feels missing in the world that *you* can contribute to?
- What are you uniquely positioned to build — because of your story?

📝 *Describe what you’re building — or what you’re committed to making real.*

---

### 🧬 d. Be You

**Essence**: Authenticity as frequency — not branding

- Who are you without performance?
- What makes you feel most *you* — in private moments?
- What mask are you ready to stop wearing?

📝 *Define what “being you” means right now — not forever. This will evolve.*

---

## 🔁 Optional Reflection Prompts

> These can be revisited quarterly or seasonally to stay aligned:
> 
- Which of the four pillars feels strongest in me right now?
- Which one feels neglected or out of sync?
- What would shift in my life if I truly honored all four, even imperfectly?
