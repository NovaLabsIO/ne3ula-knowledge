# 🏗️ Builder Path — Step 1: The Empty Blueprint

> “Before anything can be built, you must face the blankness without fear.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> Every act of creation begins with a space untouched.
> 
> 
> Not with a plan, not with a command — but with a silent decision to begin.
> 
> **The Empty Blueprint** reminds you: emptiness is not your enemy — it is your invitation.
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- If your life were an unfinished temple, what would the blueprint show so far?
- What ancient builders — mythic, legendary, or imagined — inspire you with their patience?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What foundational principles must you lay before you can build anything meaningful?
- Where are you tempted to rush ahead without fully understanding the ground you're building upon?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- What emotions arise when you face a blank canvas or an uncertain foundation?
- How do you handle the tension between desire for immediate action and the need for careful design?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- What values must be poured into the foundation — not just the surface — of what you build?
- How will you honor the invisible labor that sustains visible creation?
