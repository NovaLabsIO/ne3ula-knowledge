# 🎬 Nudge Vault Storyboard (Freedom of Mute)

---

## Panel 1 — ✍️ Sender Composes

- **Scene:** Event AR or app composer.
- **Action:** User writes a nudge (affirmation / reflection / gentle growth note).
- **Assist:** AI.d suggests kind framing; sender selects category.
- **Consent Note:** “Your message may be delayed or muted to protect the recipient.”

---

## Panel 2 — 🧠 Community Review

- **Actors:** 2–3 high-level reviewers + AI.d.
- **Checks:** Kindness, specificity, consent alignment.
- **Outcomes:**
    - ✅ Approve
    - 📝 Reframe suggestion (educational loop for sender)
    - ❌ Decline (with respectful explanation)
- **E3N reward:** reviewers earn micro-tokens for thoughtful work.

---

## Panel 3 — 🔒 Vault Storage

- Approved nudge is encrypted + stored in recipient’s Vault.
- Metadata: category, timestamp, reviewer attestations (hash on-chain).
- Status: **Locked — awaiting right state & timing.**

---

## Panel 4 — 🧘 State & Cadence Gates

- AI.d + user settings control release:
    - Only when Calm/Reflective/Rested.
    - 1–2 per day max.
    - Respects “quiet hours.”
- If overwhelmed: **“We’re holding nudges until you’re steadier.”**

---

## Panel 5 — 🔓 Ritual Unlock

- Prompt: *“A kind note is ready when you are. Breathe in. Open?”*
- Unlock ritual: 3-second breath animation.
- Message revealed with aura framing + category icon.
- Options: Save, Reflect (journal), Hide for now.

---

## Panel 6 — 📓 Integration

- Recipient can:
    - Tag to a Hem Gem principle (Mentalism, Rhythm, etc.).
    - Journal a response.
    - Explore an AI.d practice (e.g., grounding, breath).
- Message becomes part of their **soulbound Myth Archive.**

---

## Panel 7 — 🔁 Feedback to Sender (Optional)

- Sender may receive a neutral receipt: *“Your note was delivered with gratitude.”*
- No chat opens, no identity revealed.
- If reframed, sender sees mentor tip → grows too.

---

## Panel 8 — ⚙️ Controls

- **Recipient:**
    - Global mute (**Freedom of Mute**).
    - Per-category filters.
    - Per-sender limits.
    - Summary-only preview mode.
- **Sender:**
    - Draft helper, rate limits, transparency on status.
- **Reviewers:**
    - Guided ethos checklist; appeals path.

---

## Panel 9 — 🚧 Edge Handling

- Vault full → “We’ll space these over time.”
- Overwhelm detected → “Held for your well-being.”
- Muted → “Freedom of Mute is active.”

---

## Panel 10 — 🧭 Philosophy Note

> We don’t limit free speech — we route exposure with timing, consent, and care.
> 
> 
> This is **Freedom of Mute**: the right to silence until you’re ready to hear.
>
