# Comprehensive Hermetic Integration Guide

This document synthesizes the archive page that mapped the seven Hermetic
principles across the rest of the Pyramid.

## Purpose

The archive uses this page to show that the Hermetic Base is not isolated.
Each principle can be traced through later layers such as Archetypes, Elements,
Modes, Polarity, and the Alchemist.

## Canonical Interpretation

This guide should be treated as a cross-layer correspondence map.

Its function is to show how foundational principles express themselves through
the full transformation system:

- Hermetic principles provide metaphysical pattern language
- Archetypes express those patterns through orientation and role
- Elements express those patterns through inner practice paths
- Modes express those patterns through lived engagement
- Polarity reveals their tension fields
- Alchemist represents their integration

## Canonical Use

This is best used as:

- a design consistency check for future Pyramid docs
- a reflection aid for understanding cross-layer pattern recurrence
- an explanation of why the Pyramid behaves as a coherent system instead of a
  loose collection of prompts

## Stability Note

The archive's cross-mapping is sometimes partial or speculative.
Its durable contribution is the idea of holographic recurrence across layers,
not every one-to-one mapping detail.
