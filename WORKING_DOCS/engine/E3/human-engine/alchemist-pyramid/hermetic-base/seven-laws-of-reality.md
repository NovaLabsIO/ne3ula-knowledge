# Seven Laws of Reality

This document synthesizes the archive's `THE HERMETIC BASE — 7 LAWS OF REALITY`
page into canonical engine language.

## Function in the Pyramid

The Hermetic Base is the layer a participant returns to when they need to
understand the underlying structure beneath patterns, conflict, change, and
integration.

It is presented as a non-dogmatic framework:

- tools, not commandments
- mirrors, not cages
- a living structure, not a closed doctrine

## The Seven Principles

### Mentalism

Reality is shaped through consciousness and perception.

Canonical engine use:
- thought affects direction
- interpretation shapes pathing
- awareness changes experience

### Correspondence

Inner and outer patterns reflect one another.

Canonical engine use:
- relational and environmental patterns mirror internal states
- structure outside often reflects structure within

### Vibration

Everything moves, resonates, or carries frequency.

Canonical engine use:
- emotional and energetic states matter
- rhythm and signal quality influence action

### Polarity

Opposites are linked rather than fully separate.

Canonical engine use:
- tension can be held rather than prematurely resolved
- contradiction is often developmental material

### Rhythm

Transformation moves in cycles rather than straight lines.

Canonical engine use:
- return is expected
- rest and motion belong together
- repeated phases are part of mastery

### Cause and Effect

Actions and structures produce consequences.

Canonical engine use:
- habits matter
- choices compound
- systems generate outcomes

### Gender

The archive frames gender here as creative energetic polarity rather than as
social categorization.

Canonical engine use:
- initiating and receiving are both necessary
- balanced creation requires more than one kind of force

## Design Constraint

This layer should always remain an interpretive foundation.
It should not be used as a coercive belief test inside E3.
