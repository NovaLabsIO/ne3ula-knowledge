# 🕳️ Shadow Path – Step 3: The Double Life

> “We wear one face for the world, and one for ourselves. The space between is where the shadow lives.”
> 

### 🔄 RING Reflections

### 🌀 MYTHOS

- What version of you do others see—but you don’t feel is fully real?
- What parts of yourself are kept in hiding?

### 🧠 LOGOS

- Where do you act in contradiction to your own values?
- What belief keeps you in that pattern?

### ❤️ PATHOS

- What do you feel when you pretend to be someone you’re not?
- What feeling is beneath the mask?

### 🧬 ETHOS

- What truth do you wish you could live more openly?
- What would change if you gave yourself permission to show it?
