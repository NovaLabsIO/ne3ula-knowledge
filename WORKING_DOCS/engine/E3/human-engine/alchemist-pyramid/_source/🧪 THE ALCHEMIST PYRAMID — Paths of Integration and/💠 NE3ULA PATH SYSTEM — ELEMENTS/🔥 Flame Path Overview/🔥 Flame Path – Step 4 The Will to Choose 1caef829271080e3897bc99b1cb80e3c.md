# 🔥 Flame Path – Step 4: The Will to Choose

> “To choose is to create. Even the absence of choice is a decision.”
> 

### 🔄 RING Reflections

### 🌀 MYTHOS

- When in your life did you make a decision that changed everything?
- What story do you tell yourself about your ability to choose?

### 🧠 LOGOS

- What decisions are you currently avoiding?
- What internal logic or fear is keeping you from choosing?

### ❤️ PATHOS

- What emotion do you associate with regret? With courage?
- How do those emotions affect your relationship with decision-making?

### 🧬 ETHOS

- What kind of chooser do you want to be?
- What value or principle will you lean on in future decisions?
