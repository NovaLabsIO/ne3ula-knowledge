# Shadow

Shadow is the path of fear, repression, hidden protection, and reclaimed power.

## Function

Shadow reveals what has been suppressed, avoided, or outsourced to protective
behavior.

## What It Works On

- fear and shame
- protective identities
- avoidance loops
- hidden strength inside pain

## Step Progression

1. `Meeting the Darkness`
   Turn toward what is avoided instead of running from it.
2. `The Root of the Shadow`
   Trace the wound or fear beneath reactive protection.
3. `The Double Life`
   Recognize the split between outer presentation and inner truth.
4. `Reclaiming the Power`
   Identify the strength hidden inside defended material.
5. `The Shadow Ally`
   Integrate shadow into a more conscious and compassionate self.

## Canonical Role

Shadow is the path that transforms defended fragmentation into usable depth and
power.
