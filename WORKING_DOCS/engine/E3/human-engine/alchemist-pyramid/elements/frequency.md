# Frequency

Frequency is the path of resonance, emotional truth, energetic environment, and
signal calibration.

## Function

Frequency teaches the participant to read and refine their energetic and
emotional field rather than operating blindly inside it.

## What It Works On

- emotional patterning
- energetic honesty
- resonance with environments and relationships
- self-regulation and expression

## Step Progression

1. `Tuning In`
   Hear the current signal before trying to change it.
2. `Energetic Imprints`
   Notice inherited or lingering energetic residues.
3. `Resonant Environments`
   Recognize what spaces and people amplify or diminish signal.
4. `The Inner Instrument`
   Learn to regulate and transmit more consciously.
5. `The Signature Signal`
   Choose a more coherent energetic identity.

## Canonical Role

Frequency is the path that refines presence and resonance across the rest of the
Pyramid.
