# Flame

Flame is the path of will, direction, action, and consciously chosen momentum.

## Function

Flame helps the participant convert desire and intensity into focused action
rather than scattered reaction.

## What It Works On

- choice
- motivation
- resistance
- discipline
- directional action

## Step Progression

1. `The Spark`
   Recognize the recurring desire or signal asking to move.
2. `Resistance and Fire`
   See what blocks action and what that resistance protects.
3. `Direction and Discipline`
   Build supportive structure around energy.
4. `The Will to Choose`
   Reclaim decision as a creative act.
5. `The Living Flame`
   Align action, identity, and purpose into durable will.

## Canonical Role

Flame is the path that turns potential into chosen movement.
