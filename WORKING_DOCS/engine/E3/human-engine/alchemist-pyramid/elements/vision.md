# Vision

Vision is the path of future orientation, meaningful direction, and chosen
trajectory.

## Function

Vision clears fog around where the participant is going and what kind of self is
being called forward.

## What It Works On

- purpose and horizon
- future identity
- timeline alignment
- signal broadcasting into future action

## Step Progression

1. `The Fog and the Flame`
   Begin with curiosity rather than full clarity.
2. `Future Fragments`
   Gather recurring images, desires, and directions.
3. `Designing the Signal`
   Align habits and expression with the future self.
4. `Timeline Threads`
   Recognize which future is being fed through present behavior.
5. `The Future in Focus`
   Stabilize a more chosen and value-consistent direction.

## Canonical Role

Vision is the path that converts vague possibility into aligned trajectory.
