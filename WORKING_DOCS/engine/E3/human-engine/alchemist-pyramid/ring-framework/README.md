# RING Framework

RING is a four-angle interpretive lens used throughout the Alchemist Pyramid,
Identity Stack reflections, and AI.d prompting logic.

Its canonical dimensions are:

- Mythos
- Logos
- Pathos
- Ethos

## Function

RING helps translate experience into a more complete reflection by asking:

- What story is being lived or rewritten?
- What logic or pattern is operating?
- What emotions are present?
- What values or principles are at stake?

## Canonical Definitions

### Mythos

Narrative identity, symbol, role, and story pattern.

### Logos

Reasoning, systems, assumptions, structure, and mechanism.

### Pathos

Emotion, affect, felt sense, and inner charge.

### Ethos

Values, integrity, standards, and chosen way of living.

## Role in E3

RING is one of the most consistent mechanics in the archive.

It appears in:

- archetype paths
- elemental paths
- polarity reflection
- AI.d prompt design
- module-development processes such as Stretch Box

This makes RING a canonical engine lens rather than a narrow sub-tool.
