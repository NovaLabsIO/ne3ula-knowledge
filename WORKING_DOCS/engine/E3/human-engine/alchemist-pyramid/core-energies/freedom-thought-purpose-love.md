# Freedom, Thought, Purpose, Love

The archive gives this layer only a concise definition:

`Freedom, Thought, Purpose, Love`

`The forces behind WHY you act (fuel for movement)`

## Canonical Interpretation

These four should be treated as foundational motivational energies rather than
as path systems.

### Freedom

The force of autonomy, release, sovereignty, and the desire not to live
mechanically.

### Thought

The force of cognition, framing, understanding, and conscious interpretation.

### Purpose

The force of direction, meaning, and orienting commitment.

### Love

The force of connection, care, value, and relational coherence.

## Relationship to Other Layers

- Archetypes show how a participant tends to enter growth
- Core Energies describe what deeper force is driving that movement
- Elements provide the operational reflective paths where those forces are
  encountered and practiced
- Modes show how those forces are currently being lived

## Canonical Constraint

Because the archive develops this layer only briefly, these energies should be
kept conceptually clear but not over-specified beyond the source evidence.
