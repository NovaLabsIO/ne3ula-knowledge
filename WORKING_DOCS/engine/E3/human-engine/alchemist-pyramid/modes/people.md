# People

People is the mode of relationship, reflection-through-others, and social
belonging.

## Essence

Connection, mirroring, feedback, projection, belonging, and relational truth.

## What It Teaches

- others reveal hidden parts of self
- belonging requires boundaries, not self-loss
- relationship is a site of transformation

## Core Tensions

- over-identification with others
- validation dependence
- social masking
- emotional entanglement

## Canonical Role

People is the mode where identity is tested, mirrored, and refined through
contact rather than isolation.
