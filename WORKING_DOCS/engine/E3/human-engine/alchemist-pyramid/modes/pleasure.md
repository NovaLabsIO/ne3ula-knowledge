# Pleasure

Pleasure is the mode of presence, restoration, sensing, and embodied alignment.

## Essence

Joy, rest, resonance, sensation, embodiment, and receptive aliveness.

## What It Teaches

- pleasure is signal, not merely reward
- embodiment helps restore alignment
- rest is part of transformation, not its opposite

## Core Tensions

- indulgence mistaken for alignment
- guilt around rest
- numbing mistaken for nourishment

## Canonical Role

Pleasure restores contact with aliveness and helps prevent production or people
mode from becoming disembodied or extractive.
