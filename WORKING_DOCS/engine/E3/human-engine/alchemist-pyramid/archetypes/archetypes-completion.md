# Archetypes Completion

The archive closes the archetype layer with a completion page emphasizing that
the participant is not a single archetype.

## Canonical Meaning

Archetype completion does not mean graduation from role to role in a fixed line.
It means the participant has learned to recognize the spiral movement between:

- Seeker
- Builder
- Challenger
- Architect

## Canonical Lesson

The participant is not one role permanently.
They move between archetypes as different developmental demands arise.

This completion page therefore marks:

- pattern recognition across roles
- cyclical rather than static identity
- readiness to move deeper into the Pyramid
