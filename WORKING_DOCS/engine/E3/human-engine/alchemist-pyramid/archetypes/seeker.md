# Seeker

The Seeker is the archetype of first movement, longing, and signal response.

## Essence

Curiosity, longing, resonance, uncertainty, and the willingness to begin.

## What It Teaches

- follow inner signal before full certainty
- treat discomfort as an invitation to awaken
- move from passive wondering to conscious beginning

## Shadow Expressions

- escapism disguised as exploration
- addiction to seeking without embodiment
- external searching for what must be faced inwardly

## Hermetic Emphasis

- Mentalism
- Vibration
- Rhythm

## Step Progression

1. `The Restless Whisper`
   Recognize the first inner signal that something must change.
2. `The Empty Horizon`
   Face the unknown and the loss of old certainties.
3. `The Flickering Signal`
   Learn to trust resonance before proof.
4. `The Crossroads of Doubt`
   Distinguish fear from real guidance.
5. `The First True Step`
   Commit inwardly and begin with intention.

## Canonical Role in the Pyramid

The Seeker is the initiation archetype.
It opens the path but does not yet stabilize it.
