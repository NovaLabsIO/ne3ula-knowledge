# Builder

The Builder is the archetype of structure, repetition, and embodied follow-through.

## Essence

Devotion, practice, structure, repetition, and grounded creation.

## What It Teaches

- transformation is built through rhythm
- consistency shapes identity
- invisible structures matter as much as visible outcomes

## Shadow Expressions

- burnout
- rigidity
- over-identification with output
- productivity mistaken for worth

## Hermetic Emphasis

- Cause and Effect
- Correspondence
- Gender as energetic creation-through-action

## Step Progression

1. `The Empty Blueprint`
   Face the blank beginning without rushing to fill it.
2. `The Weight of the First Stone`
   Accept the real cost of beginning.
3. `The Wall That Will Not Move`
   Treat resistance as sharpening rather than as failure.
4. `The Hidden Framework`
   Recognize the invisible systems holding life together.
5. `The Sacred Repetition`
   Turn consistency into devotion rather than compulsion.

## Canonical Role in the Pyramid

The Builder grounds signal into form.
It is the archetype that teaches how intention becomes durable structure.
