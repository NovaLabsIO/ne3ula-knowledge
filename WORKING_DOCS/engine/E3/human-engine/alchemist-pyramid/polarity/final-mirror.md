# The Final Mirror

This document synthesizes the archive page `NE3ULA REFLECTIVE LAYER — POLARITY`.

## Core Meaning

Polarity is where the participant confronts divisions such as:

- control vs surrender
- productivity vs rest
- isolation vs enmeshment
- creator vs destroyer

The archive is explicit that the goal is not simply to pick the correct side.
The goal is to become capable of holding both truths without collapse.

## Canonical Role in the Pyramid

Polarity sits just before the Alchemist because it is the final reflective
tension layer before fuller integration.

## What It Teaches

- contradiction can be developmental material
- rest inside paradox matters
- certainty is often a control reflex
- integration requires witnessing both sides
