# Alchemist
Symbol: ±

## Domain

Integration  
Transformation  
Polarity

## Function

The Alchemist represents the capacity to integrate opposing forces into wisdom.

Rather than eliminating conflict, the Alchemist transforms tension into growth.

This guide helps users:

• reconcile opposing emotions  
• transform hardship into insight  
• hold multiple truths simultaneously  
• integrate shadow and strength

## Path Interaction

The Alchemist prepares users for journeys that require emotional depth and reflection.

Typical prompts may include:

What opposing forces are present within this situation?  
What truth exists in both sides?  
How might this tension be transformed into wisdom?

## Primary Teaching

Both truths exist.  
Mastery lies in learning how to hold them.
