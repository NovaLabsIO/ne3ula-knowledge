# VISION Synthesis Draft

This working document records how the VISION archive cluster was interpreted and
merged into canonical `WORKING_DOCS` documents.

Source cluster:
`NOTION_ARCHIVE/ENTER {NE3ULA}/VISION/`

## Synthesis Method Used

The cluster was read as concept groups rather than archive file boundaries.

Primary method:

archive fragment
-> interpret meaning
-> identify ontology destination
-> merge overlapping concepts
-> rewrite for clarity
-> place in canonical document

## Key Merge Decisions

- Repeated explanations of NE3ULA as mythic self-authorship infrastructure were
  consolidated into one canonical framework rather than repeated across multiple
  world docs.
- The attention-sovereignty doctrine was separated from manifesto language and
  treated as constitutional design guidance.
- The autobiographical creator manifesto was preserved as stance, not as
  founder-centered canon.
- The 4-line behavioral mantra was kept distinct from the logo-specific
  three-line symbolic mantra.
- Business-plan drafts were distilled into durable operating principles instead
  of preserving versioned product-spec language.

## Clarifications Applied

- NE3ULA is the world layer, not the entire implementation stack.
- E3 remains the transformation engine and should not be collapsed into the
  world docs.
- AXZIO remains the interface layer and should not be absorbed into VISION
  philosophy.
- Symbolic and cosmic language was preserved where meaningful, but literal
  product commitments were softened when the archive was clearly speculative.

## Remaining Ambiguities

- The boundary between symbolic artifact language and concrete product roadmap
  is still porous in the source material.
- Several business-plan concepts rely on undeveloped governance assumptions for
  creator redistribution and platform economics.
- The exact canonical place for future continuity and post-human identity
  concepts may later deserve a dedicated world or engine document if that theme
  expands.
