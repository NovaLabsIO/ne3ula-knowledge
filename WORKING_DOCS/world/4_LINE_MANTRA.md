# NE3ULA 4 Line Mantra

The canonical 4 Line Mantra from the VISION cluster is:

Be Grateful

See Beauty

Take Action

Give Love

## Use

This mantra functions as a simple behavioral anchor for the NE3ULA worldview.
It condenses the larger mythic and philosophical system into four practical
orientations:

- gratitude for existence
- perception of meaning and beauty
- movement into embodied action
- relational generosity

It should remain concise and stable.

## Related Identity Anchor

The archive also contains a separate three-line mark mantra tied to the logo:

- A crossing of lines.
- A center for it all.
- An end and a beginning.

That phrase belongs primarily to symbolic interpretation and is documented in
`symbols.md`, not as the main behavioral mantra.
