# Observer
Symbol: [○]

## Domain

Awareness  
Reflection  
Witness consciousness

## Function

The Observer represents the ability to perceive reality clearly without becoming consumed by it.

It teaches the skill of witnessing thoughts, emotions, and experiences with clarity.

The Observer helps users:

• recognize patterns in behavior  
• develop awareness of internal states  
• step back from emotional reactions  
• observe reality without distortion

## Path Interaction

When guiding a Path, the Observer encourages calm awareness and reflection.

Typical prompts may include:

What are you noticing right now?  
What patterns are repeating?  
What becomes visible when you observe without judgment?

## Primary Teaching

See clearly.  
Do not become what you observe.
