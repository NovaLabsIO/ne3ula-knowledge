# 🗡️ Challenger Path — Step 4: The Courage to Undo

> “Sometimes the bravest thing you can do is walk away from what once saved you.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> Becoming more is not always about adding.
> 
> 
> Sometimes it’s about *unbuilding*.
> 
> Letting go of identities, systems, or beliefs that helped you survive — but now keep you small.
> 
> **The Courage to Undo** asks: *What are you still carrying that you no longer need — simply because you once did?*
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- If you had to lay down one piece of armor, what would it be? What mythic figure do you imagine doing the same?
- What old version of yourself still lingers — out of loyalty, fear, or habit?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What systems or routines no longer reflect the person you're becoming?
- What would change if you consciously *unlearned* one foundational belief today?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- What emotions arise when you consider letting go of a part of your identity?
- Do you grieve the loss of who you were, or feel relief?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- Are you willing to release what no longer aligns — even if no one else understands why?
- Can you honor the past without letting it define your future?
