# 🧠 Integration Prompts

*Growth isn’t what you know — it’s what you live.*

---

## 🔗 Why Integration Matters

Awareness without action becomes noise.

Action without reflection becomes repetition.

**Integration** is the moment where insight becomes embodiment.

This module provides **precision prompts** at key moments — so the system can ask:

> “What just happened? And what does it now mean to you?”
> 

You don’t need to reflect on everything.

You just need to reflect when it matters.

---

## 🧭 When Prompts Activate

Integration Prompts are designed to surface after:

- 🔧 Friction Events
- 🧭 Mode Transitions
- 🌀 Ritual Inconsistency
- 📡 Signal Drift Alerts
- 🎯 Path Completions (e.g. Archetype step, Core Energy layer)
- 💡 Personal Epiphanies (manually triggered)

---

## 🧠 Example Prompts

> Use these manually, or allow AI.d to surface them automatically.
> 

---

### 💬 Identity Re-Alignment

- Which of my Core pillars feels strongest right now? Which one feels neglected?
- Did I abandon a personal truth to maintain comfort or control?
- Am I proud of how I responded — or am I proud of what I learned?

---

### 🌀 Friction to Flow

- What was the actual cause of that resistance — the task, or the expectation behind it?
- What emotion was trying to guide me… that I ignored?
- What pattern just revealed itself again — and what can I do differently next time?

---

### 🌊 Ritual Reflection

- What small rhythm have I been craving — but haven’t claimed?
- What ritual used to serve me that now needs to evolve or be released?

---

### 📡 Mode-Based Prompting

> If in People Mode:
> 
> 
> • Did I maintain my boundaries while being present for others?
> 
> • Where did I over-give or over-adapt?
> 

> If in Pleasure Mode:
> 
> 
> • Did I truly allow restoration — or did I judge myself the whole time?
> 

> If in Production Mode:
> 
> 
> • Am I producing from purpose… or from pressure?
>
