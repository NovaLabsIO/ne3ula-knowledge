# 🔒 Nudge Vault + “Freedom of Mute”

> Purpose: Enable custom nudges (affirmations, reflections, growth prompts) while protecting recipients from overload or harm.
> 
> 
> **Philosophy:** This system does **not** limit free speech — anyone can express themselves. What it does is provide **personal protection** by allowing the recipient to **choose if, when, and how** they hear it.
> 
> This is **Freedom of Mute**: the right to filter, pause, or delay incoming words so speech exists without forced exposure.
> 

---

## ✨ Core Tenets

- 🗣 **Free Expression Preserved** → senders can write anything (screened for safety/tone).
- 🔕 **Freedom of Mute** → recipients control what reaches them, and when.
- 🛡 **Personal Protection Layer** → no one is forced to hear something when unready.
- 🧘 **State-Aware Delivery** → nudges unlock only when the recipient is calm/reflective.
- 🌊 **Drip, Don’t Flood** → controlled cadence; no binge consumption.
- 🌑 **Consent by Design** → recipients opt-in, opt-out, or pause at will.

---

## 🧩 Roles & Flow

1. **Sender** writes a custom nudge → submits.
2. **Review Council (2–3 high-level users + AI.d)** screens → reframes if needed.
3. **Vault Storage** → nudge stored safely until conditions met.
4. **Recipient Unlocks** → at reflective moments (morning check-in, post-event, Kakun session).
5. **Freedom of Mute Options** → recipient can mute senders, categories, or all custom nudges at any time.

---

## ⚙️ Recipient Controls

- **Mute:** total silence if desired (“Freedom of Mute” active).
- **Partial Mute:** limit to affirmations only; block unsolicited advice.
- **Cadence:** max per day; quiet hours.
- **State-Gating:** deliver only during Calm/Reflective/Rested states.
- **Unlock Choice:** see summary first → open message only when ready.

---

## ⚙️ Sender Experience

- **Draft Helper:** AI.d suggests phrasing to align with kindness.
- **Review Feedback:** mentors can return notes for learning → sender grows too.
- **Transparency:** sender knows their nudge may be delayed, muted, or declined.
- **Respect:** sending is a gift, not a demand for attention.

---

## 🧠 Reviewer (Mentor) Role

- Approves / reframes / declines.
- Leaves educational notes for senders.
- Helps maintain **ecosystem health + tone**.
- Earns recognition or tokens (E3N) for thoughtful moderation.

---

## 🧾 Copy Examples

- **Vault Unlock:** “A kind note is ready when you are. Breathe first. Open?”
- **Muted State:** “You’ve chosen *Freedom of Mute*. No custom nudges will appear until you’re ready.”
- **Overwhelm Gate:** “We’re holding messages until you feel steadier.”
- **Sender Feedback:** “Can you reframe this more as appreciation than evaluation?”

---

## 🎯 Outcome

- Speech remains free.
- Recipients stay safe, sovereign, and in control.
- Custom nudges feel like **gifts that arrive at the right time**, not forced input.
- **Freedom of Mute** becomes a cultural norm → respecting each other’s right to silence is as sacred as the right to speak.
