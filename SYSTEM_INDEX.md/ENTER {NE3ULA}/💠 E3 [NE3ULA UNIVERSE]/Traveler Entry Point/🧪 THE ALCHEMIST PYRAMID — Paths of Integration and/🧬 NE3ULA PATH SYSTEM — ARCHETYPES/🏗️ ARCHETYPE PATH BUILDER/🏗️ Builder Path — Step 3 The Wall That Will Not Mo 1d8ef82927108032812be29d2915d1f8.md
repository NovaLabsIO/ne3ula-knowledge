# 🏗️ Builder Path — Step 3: The Wall That Will Not Move

> “Every structure you build will eventually test your strength.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> There will come a moment when what you're building resists you.
> 
> 
> Progress halts. Obstacles rise. Doubt creeps in.
> 
> **The Wall That Will Not Move** reveals this truth: resistance is not failure — it is the sharpening of purpose.
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- Imagine yourself as a mythic builder. What trial do you face that makes you question your blueprint?
- What story would be told about the wall you chose to face — or walk away from?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What isn’t working right now — and why?
- Is the obstacle one of structure, effort, timing, or alignment?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- How do you respond emotionally when your progress feels blocked?
- Where do frustration or discouragement show up in your body — and what do they need from you?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- What would it look like to face this obstacle with integrity rather than force?
- How can you return to your foundational “why” to find the strength to continue?
