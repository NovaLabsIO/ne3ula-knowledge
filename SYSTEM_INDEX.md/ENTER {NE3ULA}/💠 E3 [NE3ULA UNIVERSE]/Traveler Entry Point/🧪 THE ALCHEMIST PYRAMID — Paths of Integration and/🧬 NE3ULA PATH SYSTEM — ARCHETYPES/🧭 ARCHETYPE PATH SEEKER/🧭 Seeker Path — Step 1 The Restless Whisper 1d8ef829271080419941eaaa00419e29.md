# 🧭 Seeker Path — Step 1: The Restless Whisper

> “You were not born to stay asleep. Somewhere inside you, the stars still murmur your name.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> Before the journey begins, there is a stirring. A whisper.
> 
> 
> You cannot see it yet — but you can *feel* it.
> 
> **The Restless Whisper** is the first invitation:
> 
> a knowing without language, a discontent without cause.
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- What was the first moment you remember feeling like you were meant for *more* than your current life?
- If your soul sent you a signal, what form would it take — a sound, a vision, a dream?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What parts of your life currently feel "too small" for who you are becoming?
- What assumptions about yourself or the world have you stopped believing — even if you haven’t admitted it yet?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- Where do you feel the ache of longing most strongly in your body?
- How does it feel to sit in the space between dissatisfaction and action?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- If you honor the whisper inside you, what kind of life would you be stepping into?
- What sacred responsibility comes with awakening to "more"?
