# 🧭 Seeker Path — Step 4: The Crossroads of Doubt

> “Every true path demands you question it before you can walk it fully.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> Doubt is not the enemy — it is the guardian of transformation.
> 
> 
> It asks: *Are you ready? Are you willing?*
> 
> **The Crossroads of Doubt** is where old fears and new visions collide — and you must choose which voice to follow.
>

- Imagine yourself at a crossroads where every road is shrouded in mist. What instinct rises first?
- What mythic heroes faced doubt — and how did they decide to move forward?

- What evidence do you already have that you are capable of more than your doubt suggests?

- What emotions arise when you imagine choosing the unknown over the familiar?

- How would your future self — the one who crossed the threshold — advise you right now?
