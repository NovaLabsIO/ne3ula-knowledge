## 🧭 Overview

**AI.d** is your responsive mirror — tracking patterns, surfacing prompts, and adapting over time based on your Identity Core and Engine activity. It never replaces you. It amplifies your signal.

You can:

- Reflect with Signal Reports
- Notice trends with the Trend Mapper
- Drop Signal Cards as milestones
- Export your evolution
- Or just check in when you feel a little... off

All tools are optional and modular. Activate only what feels aligned.
