## 🧬 What Is It?

The Signal Card Drop-In allows you to **insert your Signal Cards into your Trend Mapper timeline**.

It turns your journey into a **visible mythology** — a personalized map of who you were becoming at key points in time.

## 📅 Where Cards Can Be Dropped

You can drop a Signal Card into:

- Any week on your **Trend Mapper** timeline
- The day of a **Ritual breakthrough or collapse**
- The completion of a **Path** or **Core Protocol reset**
- A **Drift recovery point**
- A personal moment you define as significant — no justification needed
