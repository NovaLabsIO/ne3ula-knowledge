Your submission is reviewed by the NE3ULA team or trusted members of the community. If accepted:

- Your **Contributor Orb** evolves
- You earn a **Badge of Resonance** (e.g. Signal Sender, Myth Weaver, Architect)
- Your name is added to the **Genesis Log**
