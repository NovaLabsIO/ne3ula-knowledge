## 🧬 The Core Framework: The Alchemist Pyramid

At the heart of NE3ULA is the **Alchemist Pyramid** —

a multi-layered journey through the deepest aspects of self, structured around:

1. **Hermetic Principles** — the laws of reality
2. **Archetypes** — the roles you move through
3. **Core Energies** — forces within you to align
4. **Modes** — how you engage with the world
5. **Polarity** — the mirror of tension
6. **The Alchemist** — the return to signal

All of this rests upon the **Four Pillars of the Self**:

- 🧍 **Body** — the vessel
- 🧠 **Mind** — the interpreter
- ❤️ **Heart** — the compass
- 🔮 **Spirit** — the signal

And guided by the **RING**:

🌀 **Mythos · Logos · Pathos · Ethos**

Your lenses of meaning, motion, emotion, and alignment.
