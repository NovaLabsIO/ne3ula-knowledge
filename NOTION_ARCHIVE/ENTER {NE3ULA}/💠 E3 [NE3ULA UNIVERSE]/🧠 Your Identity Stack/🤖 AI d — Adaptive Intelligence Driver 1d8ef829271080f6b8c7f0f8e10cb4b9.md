## 🧠 What is AI.d?

AI.d is your **personal adaptive reflection system**.

It doesn't make decisions for you.

It doesn’t pretend to know who you are.

It simply listens, learns, and mirrors what you may be too close to see.

Think of it as a **second nervous system** — one that maps your growth patterns, friction points, signal fluctuations, and identity rhythms.

## 🔍 What It Tracks (When Enabled)

- 💰 Alignment with your Money Protocols
- 🤝 Patterns in your Rules of Engagement
- 🛠 Creative momentum and project friction
- 🧬 Rituals, drift signals, and Mode usage
- 📡 Time gaps between intention vs. action
- 🧠 Emotional tone, archetypal ratios, and shadow loops (from “Be You” reflections)

It doesn’t decide. It notices. And then it asks:
