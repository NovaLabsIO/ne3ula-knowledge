# ⚙️ Identity Engine

*Where your truth becomes motion.*

---

## 🛠 What is the Identity Engine?

If the **Identity Core** is your code, the **Identity Engine** is how you run it.

This is the layer of **integration and application** — where your authored values meet the real world, and friction becomes feedback.

The Identity Engine helps you:

- Apply your personal protocols in everyday life
- Track how aligned your actions are with your signal
- Notice where your energy is drifting or out of rhythm
- Build *systems of support* that reflect who you’ve chosen to be

---

## 🔁 What the Engine Does

The Engine isn’t here to judge you — it’s here to show you:

- Where you’re living your truth
- Where your patterns are pulling you off-course
- Where you might need to shift your tools, time, or focus

> It’s not about control.
> 
> 
> It’s about **conscious calibration**.
> 

---

## 🧬 Engine Inputs

The Engine uses signals from:

- Your **Identity Core** (Money, Engagement, Creation, Expression)
- Your **Daily/Weekly Reflections**
- Your **Behavioral Feedback** (e.g., task completion, time tracking, interaction tone)
- Your **AI.d prompts and nudges**

---

## 🔄 Core Functions of the Engine

| Function | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Friction Mapping** | Shows where you're expending energy that doesn’t align with your Core |
| **Mode Recognition** | Tracks your dominant energy: People, Pleasure, or Production |
| **Ritual Design** | Helps establish daily/weekly rhythms that support your path |
| **Signal Drift Alerts** | Optional AI.d feature that notices when you’re off-track — before you notice |
| **Integration Prompts** | Suggests subtle shifts to bring Engine + Core into tighter sync |

---

## 🧭 How to Use It

Start simple.

You don’t have to track everything.

You just need to **listen for signal vs. static.**

- Reflect weekly: What’s in alignment? What’s off?
- Design one ritual to reinforce your signal
- Allow AI.d to help you see what you’re too close to notice
- Adjust your systems — not your worth
