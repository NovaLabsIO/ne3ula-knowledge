## 🎭 What It Is

This module lets you **track, visualize, and reflect on your evolving relationship** with the four archetypes in the NE3ULA path:

- 🧭 Seeker – The Call to Begin
- 🏗️ Builder – The Power of Form
- 🗡️ Challenger – The Spark of Disruption
- 🧠 Architect – The Vision to Guide

It’s not about choosing “your type.”

It’s about recognizing **which role is most alive in you right now** — and how that has changed across time.
