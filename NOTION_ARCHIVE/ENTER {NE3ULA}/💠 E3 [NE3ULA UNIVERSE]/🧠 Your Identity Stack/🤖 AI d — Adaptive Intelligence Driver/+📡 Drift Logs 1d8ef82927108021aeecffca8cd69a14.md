# +📡 Drift Logs

*Notice the distance. Celebrate the return.*

---

## 🌀 What is Signal Drift?

Drift is when your actions, energy, or expression **move out of sync** with your Identity Core.

It doesn’t mean failure — it means you’re human.

And the ability to notice and **return** is the essence of mastery.

Drift Logs let you:

- Track moments of misalignment
- Record triggers, patterns, and causes
- Identify recovery methods that work for you
- Reflect on the meaning of each disconnection
- Learn how your signal moves, cycles, and resurfaces

---

## 🧭 What Drift Looks Like

Common drift symptoms:

- ❌ Ritual drop-off
- 😶 Emotional flattening
- 😤 Chronic resistance to aligned tasks
- 📉 Mode imbalance without transition
- 🧍 Self-abandonment in conflict or collaboration
- 💬 Repetitive internal scripts (“I’m behind,” “I don’t care,” “What’s the point?”)
