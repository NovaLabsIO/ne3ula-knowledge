# 🪞 Mirror Prompts

*Signal-aware questions from the adaptive mirror within.*

---

## 🧠 What Are Mirror Prompts?

These are **contextual questions** triggered by your actions, skips, language, or patterns.

Their goal isn’t correction — it’s clarity.

They emerge when something subtle shifts:

- A skipped ritual
- A journal tone change
- A recurring phrase or avoidance loop
- A noticeable mode imbalance
- A drift from your active Archetype or Core Pillar

You can respond, ignore, schedule for later, or mute them entirely.

---

## 🧭 Prompt Activation Sources

| Trigger | Example Prompt |
| --- | --- |
| 🧱 Ritual Inconsistency | “You’ve skipped your Build Check-In 3x. Are you avoiding structure or craving freedom?” |
| 📉 Mode Imbalance | “You’ve been in Production Mode 17 of the last 20 days. What’s being left unfelt?” |
| 🌀 Drift Flag | “You stopped logging after saying 'I don’t even know who I am right now.' Want to look closer?” |
| 📋 Language Pattern | “You’ve used 'I should' five times this week. Whose script is that?” |
| 🔁 Repetitive Friction | “This is the third week this challenge showed up. What’s the pattern hiding underneath?” |

## 🧘 Prompt Tone Options

You can choose how AI.d speaks to you:

- 🪞 *Gentle Reflection* — curious, soft, invitational
- 🔍 *Sharp Insight* — honest, unfiltered, piercing clarity
- 🎭 *Poetic Riddle* — abstract, mystical, dream-logic
- 🛠 *Direct Utility* — practical, clean, short-form

🧠 Example (same moment, different tones):

> Gentle: “What might your body be trying to tell you right now?”
> 
> 
> Sharp: “You’re avoiding the truth by staying busy.”
> 
> Poetic: “You speak in schedules, but your soul wants to sing.”
> 
> Direct: “You’ve skipped 4 rituals. What needs to change?”
>
