# 🔧 Friction Mapping

*Notice the static. Honor the signal.*

---

## 🧱 What is Friction?

Friction is anything that:

- Drains your energy disproportionately
- Creates internal resistance or dread
- Pulls you out of alignment with your Identity Core
- Feels like you’re moving through mud instead of flow

Friction isn’t failure — it’s feedback.

The goal isn’t to eliminate all discomfort.

The goal is to **discern what’s misaligned vs what’s growing you.**

---

## 🔍 How to Map Friction

### 🧭 Step 1: Spot the Symptoms

Where in your week did you feel…

- 😤 Resistance
- 😓 Overwhelm
- 😞 Disappointment or burnout
- 😶 Apathy or emotional numbness
- 🤐 Self-silencing

📝 *List any recent examples below. Be honest. The smallest moments often reveal the biggest truths.*

---

### 🔍 Step 2: Pattern the Source

What kind of friction is it?

| Type | Description | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Energetic** | Your body says no | You keep delaying a task even though it’s “important” |
| **Emotional** | A wound is being poked | You feel unseen in a group you used to enjoy |
| **Cognitive** | Belief doesn’t match action | You’re saying yes to something you don’t value |
| **Creative** | Vision doesn’t match structure | The project no longer reflects your why |
| **Relational** | Boundaries crossed | You’re shrinking around someone you admire |

📝 *Tag each example from Step 1 with one or more friction types.*

---

### 🔍 Step 3: Compare to Your Identity Core

Now ask:

- Which part of my Identity Core is this friction connected to?
    - 💰 Money
    - 🤝 Engagement
    - 🛠 Building
    - 🧬 Being Me
- Is the friction highlighting a value I’ve outgrown… or one I’ve abandoned?
- Is this a call to **shift my system** — or **revisit my code**?

📝 *Make a quick connection note for each item above.*

---

## 🧠 Friction Isn’t Bad

> "Alignment doesn’t mean ease.
> 
> 
> It means clarity."
> 

Some friction is creative tension — it's the pressure that shapes you.

Some is destructive — it’s the noise drowning your signal.

**Your job is to tell the difference.**
