**Relationship** · **Social Balance** · Reflection · Belonging · Projection

### **Function in the System**:

**People Mode** is where you encounter your Self — **through the mirror of others**.

It is the mode of **connection, friction, resonance, and revelation**.

Whether in community, partnership, conflict, or support, People Mode brings out what is **buried, disowned, or activated** in you.

You do not exist in isolation in NE3ULA — and this Mode reveals how **identity is shaped in relation**.

---

### **Tensions / Blockages**:

- Losing identity to roles, groupthink, or relationships
- Blaming others for your discomfort
- Constantly seeking validation or fearing rejection
- Avoiding connection due to past pain or betrayal
- Idealizing people as saviors or enemies
- Mistaking reflection for truth without discernment

---

### 🛌 **The Role of Rest in People Mode**

Rest in this Mode isn’t stillness for the body — it’s **space for the self**.

It’s the ability to step back from social feedback loops, emotional entanglement, and the constant mirroring of identity.

Rest here looks like:

- **Energetic Boundaries** — Recognizing when connection becomes depletion
- **Relational Overexertion** — Avoiding burnout from people-pleasing, emotional labor, or social masking
- **Withdrawal with Purpose** — Choosing solitude to recentralize your internal signal
- **Recovery from Overidentification** — Releasing roles and expectations that distort self-perception

> In People Mode, rest is not absence — it’s alignment.
> 
> 
> You’re not walking away from others. You’re walking **back to yourself**.
> 

---

### 🔄 **Transitional Indicators**

> How do you know it’s time to shift out of People Mode?
> 
- You feel **emotionally overstimulated or entangled**
- Your sense of identity feels blurred or scattered
- You’re adjusting your truth to match the room
- You crave solitude but feel guilty about needing it
- You're responding more to perceived expectations than to internal alignment

This may signal a shift toward:

- 🌟 **Pleasure** — to reconnect with embodied self-trust
- 📈 **Production** — to reclaim clarity, agency, and forward movement

### **Pyramid Integration**:

**Archetypes**:

- **Seeker** learns through mentorship, inspiration, or loss
- **Builder** applies lessons from others to real-world form
- **Challenger** often *is* the other — the one who breaks your illusion
- **Architect** sees social structures and identity webs

**Core Energies**:

- **Mirror** — Activated constantly in People Mode
- **Shadow** — Revealed through projection, envy, admiration
- **Frequency** — Resonance between individuals
- **Vision** — Sensing shared direction or divergent values
- **Flame** — Attraction, conflict, desire, and inspiration

**Modes**:

- People Mode is the bridge between Pleasure (Self-experience) and Production (World-shaping)
- **Rest** here often means a boundary, a pause, or quiet integration between mirrors
