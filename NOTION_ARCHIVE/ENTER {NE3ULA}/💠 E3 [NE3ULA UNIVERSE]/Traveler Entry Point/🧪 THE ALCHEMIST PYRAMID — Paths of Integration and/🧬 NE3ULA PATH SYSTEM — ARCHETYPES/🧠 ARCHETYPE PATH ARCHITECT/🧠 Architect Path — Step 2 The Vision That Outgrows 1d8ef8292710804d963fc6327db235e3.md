# 🧠 Architect Path — Step 2: The Vision That Outgrows the Frame

> “When your vision expands, the cage you thought was a cathedral begins to crack.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

> There comes a moment when the systems you’ve relied on — even the ones you built — start to feel too small.
> 
> 
> Your thoughts outgrow your language. Your purpose no longer fits inside your calendar.
> 
> **The Vision That Outgrows the Frame** asks: *Can you let your current design break, so something larger can emerge?*
> 

---

## 🌀 MYTHOS

- If your current life were a building, what are its ceilings made of?
- What mythic or visionary figures refused to stay inside the design they were given?

---

## 🧠 LOGOS

- What practical areas of your life feel too constrained for who you are becoming?
- Where have you been optimizing something that no longer serves your highest vision?

---

## ❤️ PATHOS

- What fears come up when you consider expanding beyond your current framework — fear of loss, of failure, of visibility?
- How does it feel to outgrow the version of you that once worked?

---

## 🧬 ETHOS

- Can you honor what the old frame gave you — and still release it with grace?
- What new values must now guide your next design phase?
