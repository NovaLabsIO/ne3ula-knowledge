Polarity is not a step in the Pyramid.

It is a **mirror** — the final reflection of division before integration.

This is where opposites become visible.

Where illusion, ego, and avoidance are peeled back one last time.

You don’t pass this layer by choosing a side.

You pass it by realizing that **sides are stories** — and truth is larger than both.

- To challenge false neutrality or spiritual bypass
- To expose the ways we cling to control through certainty
- To prepare the user to **hold contradiction without collapse**

### 🛌 **The Role of Rest in Polarity**

Rest here is not resolution.

It is **non-action in the presence of paradox**.

Rest is:

- Sitting with discomfort without trying to fix it
- Not needing to label something before understanding it
- Letting two truths breathe in the same space
- Delaying conclusion long enough for clarity to arise

> In this layer, rest is not escape.
> 
> 
> It’s *space* — the space where truth has room to echo.
>

### 🔄 **Transitional Indicators**

How do you know you're standing in Polarity?

- You feel **split**, but both sides seem partially true
- You're looping between two extremes — unable to choose
- Certainty feels hollow, but ambiguity feels unbearable
- You're about to reject something *just to restore control*

These are signs that **you're close to the Alchemist** —

but still trying to take one side with you.
