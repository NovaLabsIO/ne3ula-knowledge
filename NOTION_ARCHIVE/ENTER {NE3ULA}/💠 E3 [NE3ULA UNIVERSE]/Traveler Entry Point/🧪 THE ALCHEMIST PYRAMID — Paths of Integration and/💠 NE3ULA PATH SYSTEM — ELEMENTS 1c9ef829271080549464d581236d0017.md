### 🌀 What Are the Core Energies?

The Core Energies are not roles you play or actions you take —

They are **forces** that move through you.

They shape your thoughts, behaviors, desires, reactions, and patterns.

These five energies represent the **interior framework** of your identity.

Understanding them allows you to **see what drives you**,

and to rewire how you engage with the rest of the system.

---

### 📍 Where They Appear in the Pyramid

The Core Energies layer comes **after Archetypes** —

Once you’ve explored the mythic roles you inhabit,

you are now ready to tune into the **forces behind those roles**.

This is not the beginning of your journey.

It’s your **first real reflection on your internal wiring**.

---

### 🪞 The 5 Core Energies

- 🔥 **Flame** — Your drive, willpower, and initiatory spark
- 🪞 **Mirror** — Your self-perception, worth, and reflection in others
- 🌑 **Shadow** — Your hidden fears, wounds, and repressed truths
- 🎶 **Frequency** — Your rhythm, energy state, and capacity for flow
- 👁️ **Vision** — Your purpose, values, and ability to see beyond now

Each energy can become **distorted or suppressed**,

but all are essential to wholeness.

---

### 🔄 What This Layer Teaches

You’ll learn how to:

- Recognize energetic imbalances
- Name and navigate your inner conflicts
- Understand the *forces beneath your patterns*
- Begin shifting from reaction → response → resonance

You don’t need to eliminate your Shadow.

You don’t need to live only in Flame or Vision.

The goal is **dynamic alignment** —

Knowing which energy is speaking… and whether it’s telling the truth.

---

### 🧬 The RING Model Returns

In this layer, you'll use the **RING lenses** again:

- 🌀 **Mythos** — What narrative shaped this energy in you?
- 🧠 **Logos** — What patterns or logic does it follow?
- ❤️ **Pathos** — What emotions fuel or distort it?
- 🧬 **Ethos** — How does it connect to your core truth?
