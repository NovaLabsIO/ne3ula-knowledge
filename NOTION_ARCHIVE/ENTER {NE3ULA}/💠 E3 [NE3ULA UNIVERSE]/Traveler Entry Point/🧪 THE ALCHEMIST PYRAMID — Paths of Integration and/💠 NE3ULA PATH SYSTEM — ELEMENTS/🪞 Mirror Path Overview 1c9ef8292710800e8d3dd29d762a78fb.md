**🔍 What Is the Mirror Path?**

The **Mirror Path** is the first step on your NE3ULA journey.

It invites you to face yourself honestly—beyond filters, roles, and projections.

This is the **foundation of all transformation**.

Through 5 carefully crafted steps, you will reflect deeply through the four lenses of the Ring Framework:

•	🌀 **Mythos** – Your story and identity

•	🧠 **Logos** – The logic behind your patterns

•	❤️ **Pathos** – The emotions beneath your reactions

•	🧬 **Ethos** – The values that define your truth

---

**🔄 What You Will Explore**

•	The story you’ve been telling yourself—and the story that wants to emerge

•	The emotions you hide and the logic behind your reactions

•	The values you claim vs. the ones you actually live

•	The parts of you that were lost, suppressed, or waiting to be reclaimed

---

**🧠 Mirror + Your AI.dentity**

Each step you take helps train your NE3ULA AI.dentity—a digital reflection built from your truths, chosen beliefs, and emotional clarity.

By completing the Mirror Path, your AI.d will begin to understand how to support you in **alignment with who you really are**, not who the world expects you to be.
