# 🪞 Mirror Path: Step 1 — Facing the Reflection

> “Before you change the world—or yourself—you must first see what’s really there. This is the first crack in the illusion.”
> 

---

**🔄 RING Reflections**

Use these 4 lenses to reflect on your current identity.

---

**🌀 MYTHOS**

•	What story have you been telling yourself about who you are?

•	What chapter of your myth are you in right now?

---

**🧠 LOGOS**

•	What patterns or principles seem to define your current identity?

•	Where do you notice consistency or contradiction in your actions?

---

**❤️ PATHOS**

•	What emotions come up when you reflect on how others see you?

•	What emotions are hidden from others? From yourself?

---

**🧬 ETHOS**

•	Does how you live match what you value?

•	What behavior do you allow that goes against your truth?
