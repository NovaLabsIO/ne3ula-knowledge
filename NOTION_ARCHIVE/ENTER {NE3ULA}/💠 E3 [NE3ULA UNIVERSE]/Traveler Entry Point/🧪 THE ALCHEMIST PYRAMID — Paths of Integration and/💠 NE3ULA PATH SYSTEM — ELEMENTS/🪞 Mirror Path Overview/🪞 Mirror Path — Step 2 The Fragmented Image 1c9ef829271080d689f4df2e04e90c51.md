# 🪞 Mirror Path — Step 2: The Fragmented Image

> “What you see is not always what is. The mirror doesn’t lie—but it does reflect distortion.”
>

**🔄 RING Reflections**

---

**🌀 MYTHOS**

•	What parts of yourself do you try to hide or ignore?

•	If someone else wrote your story, what would they say about your contradictions?

---

**🧠 LOGOS**

•	Where are the gaps between how you act and what you believe?

•	What feedback loops or behaviors do you keep repeating?

•	What feelings are difficult to accept about yourself?

•	What emotions surface when you feel misunderstood?

---

**🧬 ETHOS**

•	What masks have you worn to be accepted or stay safe?

•	Are they still serving you—or holding you back?
