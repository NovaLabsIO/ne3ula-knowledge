# 🔥 Flame Path – Step 2: Resistance and Fire

> “Every fire meets resistance. But some fuel it, and some smother it.”
> 

### 🔄 RING Reflections

### 🌀 MYTHOS

- What story do you tell yourself when you stop short of action?
- What role does resistance play in your current identity?

### 🧠 LOGOS

- What patterns stop you from taking action?
- Are they protecting something—or hiding something?

### ❤️ PATHOS

- What emotion comes up most when you're about to take action—and don't?
- Where in your body does that emotion live?

### 🧬 ETHOS

- What truth are you afraid of proving or disproving through action?
- What principle can guide you through resistance?
