### **🔮 What Is the Vision Path?**

The **Vision Path** clears the fog.

It reveals your future self, your destination, and the patterns calling you forward.

It is the space between clarity and becoming—the light on the horizon that begins within.

> You’ve met your shadow. You’ve activated your will.
> 

> Now, it’s time to decide where you’re going.
> 

---

### **🔄 What You Will Explore**

- The difference between a goal and a vision
- Your unique definition of “success” and “impact”
- What your future self needs from you now
- The steps that bring the dream closer

---

### **🧠 Vision + Your AI.dentity**

The Vision Path teaches your AI.d to recognize your long-term desires and direction.

As you clarify where you’re going, your AI.d refines its ability to nudge, support, and align opportunities that match your chosen path.

It becomes your compass when you forget where you’re heading.
