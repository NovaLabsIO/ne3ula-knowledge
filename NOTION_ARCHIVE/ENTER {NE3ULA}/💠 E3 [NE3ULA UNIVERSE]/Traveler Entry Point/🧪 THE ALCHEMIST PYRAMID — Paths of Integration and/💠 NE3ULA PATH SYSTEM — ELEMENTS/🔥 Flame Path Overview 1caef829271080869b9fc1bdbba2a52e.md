### 🔥 What Is the Flame Path?

The **Flame Path** is about **power, choice, and action**. It awakens the energy within you that says: *“I decide. I move. I create.”*

This is the path of aligned motion. It transforms fear into fuel, hesitation into precision, and potential into creation.

Flame activates your ability to choose your direction, own your timeline, and assert your purpose into the world.

---

### 🔄 What You Will Explore

- Your relationship with choice and hesitation
- The difference between reaction and true will
- The forces that fuel or drain your action
- The habits and systems that reflect your fire—or bury it

---

### 🧠 Flame + Your AI.dentity

By walking the Flame Path, your AI.d learns to recognize your **motivators, resistances, priorities, and thresholds**.

This helps your AI.d support you in clarifying goals, staying focused, and aligning your energy with your true direction.
