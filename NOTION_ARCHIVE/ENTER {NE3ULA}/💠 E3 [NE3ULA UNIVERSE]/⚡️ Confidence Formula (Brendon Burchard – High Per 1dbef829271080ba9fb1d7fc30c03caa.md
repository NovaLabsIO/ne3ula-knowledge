# ⚡️ Confidence Formula (Brendon Burchard – High Performance Habits)

• Confidence Formula · Applied Path

> Confidence = Curiosity × (Competence + Congruence + Connection)
> 

### 1. **Curiosity**

The amplifier. A curious mind stays open, adaptable, and engaged. Curiosity fuels the desire to grow and learn—without it, confidence becomes stagnant.

### 2. **Competence**

Skill mastery. The more you *know* and can *do*, the more confident you feel. Competence comes from consistent practice, feedback, and performance under pressure.

### 3. **Congruence**

Authentic alignment. Confidence deepens when your actions align with your values and identity. When you *live your truth*, you eliminate inner friction.

### 4. **Connection**

Support system. Relationships reinforce belief. Confidence is magnified when you feel seen, supported, and part of something bigger.

---

### **Insight for E3 (Empowered Energy Engine)**

This formula maps directly to the inner state of empowered presence. In E3, confidence isn't a trait—it's a *state* activated by curiosity and supported by the inner alignment (congruence), mastery (competence), and resonance with others (connection).

Use this to recalibrate when confidence wavers:

- Reignite curiosity
- Refine skills
- Realign actions
- Reconnect with others
