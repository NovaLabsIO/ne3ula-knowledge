## 🧩 VI. Path Step Layout Using the RING Model

Each step in a NE3ULA Path includes:

1. **Prompt or Activity** aligned with a Pyramid concept
2. **RING Reflections:**
    - **Mythos**: What mythic theme or personal narrative is reflected in this step?
    - **Logos**: What universal principle or internal logic is at play here?
    - **Pathos**: What feelings emerge from engaging with this challenge?
    - **Ethos**: Does this resonate with your values? How does it affect your integrity?
3. **AI.dentity Feedback Loop**
    - After the step, AI.dentity offers insights, asks questions, and updates its internal map
    - e.g., If a user logs fear of being seen, AI.d might suggest reviewing Mirror or Shadow steps
4. **Optional Path Rating or Review**
    - Allows reflection and Path refinement
